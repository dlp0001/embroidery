Summary: Full refund available within 14 days of purchase, no questions asked.

We want you to feel completely confident purchasing our online embroidery course. This Refund Policy explains your rights as a customer.

1. 14-Day Refund Guarantee

You are entitled to a full refund if your refund request is submitted within 14 calendar days of your purchase date. No questions asked.

We process all refund requests promptly and without requiring any justification from you.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us via email at info@re-create.art within 14 days of purchase. Include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Email address used at purchase
  • Date of purchase

We will process your refund within 3 business days of receiving your request.

3. Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed through Paddle, our authorised payment provider. Please allow 5–10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or card issuer.

4. After 14 Days

Refund requests submitted after the 14-day window will be considered on a case-by-case basis at our sole discretion.

5. Technical Issues

If you experience any technical difficulties accessing the course content, please contact us at info@re-create.art before requesting a refund. We will make every effort to resolve any technical issues promptly.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. The policy in effect at the time of your purchase applies to your order. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
