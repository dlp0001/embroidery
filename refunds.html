Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Varvara Perlin, ID 345921738

We want you to feel confident purchasing our online embroidery course. This Refund Policy explains your rights and the conditions under which refunds are granted.

Summary: Full refund available within 7 days of purchase if no live sessions have been attended and no digital materials have been downloaded.

1. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if all of the following conditions are met:

  • Your refund request is submitted within 7 calendar days of your purchase date
  • You have not attended any live Zoom sessions
  • You have not downloaded any digital materials (PDF schemes, templates, etc.)

2. Refund Schedule

Time since purchase Refund amount
0–7 days (no sessions attended, no materials downloaded) 100% refund
0–7 days (sessions attended or materials downloaded) No refund
After 7 days No refund

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us via the Telegram channel provided in your course confirmation email within the eligible period. Include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Email address used at purchase
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for the refund request (optional but appreciated)

We will review your request and respond within 3 business days.

4. Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed through the original payment method via Paddle. Please allow 5–10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

5. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will not be granted in the following situations:

  • The 7-day window has passed
  • Any live session has been attended
  • Digital materials have been accessed or downloaded
  • The request is made after the course has concluded
  • Violation of the course Terms & Conditions

6. Technical Issues

If you experience technical difficulties accessing the course content, please contact us before requesting a refund. We will make every reasonable effort to resolve technical issues promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page. The policy in effect at the time of your purchase applies to your order.

8. Contact

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please reach out via the Telegram channel provided in your confirmation email.
